About Azuvila

Azuvila supports strategic growth and partnership development for small businesses entering the government contracting market. Our advisory services include business development strategy, capture management, and proposal development.

Whether conducting market trend analysis, leading a business development audit, or supporting positioning efforts, our focus is enhancing your visibility and advancing your ability to grow. We improve systems, strengthen processes, and provide guidance to transform your capture and proposal structure.

Founder: Maricarmen Smith-Martinez is a strategic growth and partnerships executive with extensive cross-sector leadership experience in government contracting, international development, and nonprofit strategy. She specializes in organizational growth, partnership development, and mission-aligned portfolio expansion.
